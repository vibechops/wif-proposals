# Deck conventions

How WIF proposal decks are structured. Match these patterns when drafting `deck-outline.md`.

Gold standard: `google/deck-outline.md`.

---

## Slide count

- **14 slides + appendix** for modular partner proposals
- **~10 A4 pages** for print/PDF (`a4-pdf-outline.md`)

---

## Narrative arc

1. Title + one-line promise
2. Modular overview (or program map)
3. Flow diagram (mermaid)
4-7. One slide per module
8. Venues / reach
9. Timeline
10. Why WIF
11. Why partner
12. Budget options (A vs B)
13. Success metrics / outcomes
14. The ask (split: partner vs WIF vs WIF internal decision)

---

## Tone

- Confident, specific, scannable
- Tables over paragraphs on slide content
- "Modular" language when US/global approval is involved
- Separate confirmed vs requested items on the final ask slide

---

## Mermaid

Use simple left-to-right or top-down flowcharts in slide 3. Keep node labels short.

---

## Appendix

- Budget summary (pointer to budget-outlook.md)
- Tool/pricing tables if relevant
- Responsible AI / content rules if partner requires them

---

## Deliverables chain

```
event-spec.md (filled) → deck-outline.md → a4-pdf-outline.md → 06-deliverables/
```

Optional: `07-viewer/` interactive HTML deck (see `google/viewer/`).
