## Why independent options (not bundled tiers)

Rabia confirmed (Jun 2026): these are **not tiers**. Dell or the agency picks one of three programs.

- Each program has a distinct impact narrative (develop / distribute / employ)
- Bundling obscured the choice and created artificial tier math ($265K, $595K)
- Independent pricing lets EMG match Dell's priority to a single clear ask

## Negotiation strategy

1. Present all three options neutrally; let Dell/EMG choose based on brand priority
2. **Lab at $150K** and **Exhibition at $150K** are both healthy margin programs
3. **Internship at $200K/year** is a relationship / impact play; do not go below $180K without reducing scope (smaller cohort, fewer departments)
4. **In-film branding** (Sikhya / Raonaq) is a separate conversation; don't bundle into WIF program fee
5. Multi-year internship: $200K/year per cohort; optional Years 2–3 at same rate
