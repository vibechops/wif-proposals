## Page 3 — Where you'd plug in

**Exposure** ($75K–$200K per delegation)  
Walk Cannes or TIFF with the filmmakers we send abroad. Cannes (producers) and TIFF (directors) done. Next: Sundance, Berlin, global mentorship exchange.

**Education** (Fellowship $100K–$250K · Labs $50K–$150K · Masterclasses $25K–$50K)  
Masterclasses, fellowship cohort (15–20), Soho House partnership, labs for writers and directors — skill meets community.

**Employment** (Internships $50K–$100K · Grants $50K–$150K/yr)  
First stipend on a set, a short off the ground — one woman, one job, one project at a time.

**Exhibition** (Festival $200K–$500K · Conclave $100K–$250K · WIF House $500K+)  
Red carpet with industry leaders; travelling festival (10 films, 10 cities); conclave; WIF House as permanent creative home.
